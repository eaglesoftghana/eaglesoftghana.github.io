Frequently Asked Questions

Are your systems compatible with local payment gateways (e.g., Mobile Money)?

Yes, a key advantage of Eaglesoft Ghana is our commitment to localization. All our relevant SaaS platforms integrate smoothly with popular Ghanaian payment gateways like Mobile Money and local banks.

How is data security handled, especially for sensitive data like in Clinic Plus?

We prioritize data privacy and security, adhering to best international practices. We ensure data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, with robust access controls and local backup protocols tailored to regulatory requirements in Ghana.

We offer dedicated local support via phone, email, and on-site consultations (depending on your plan). Our support team is based in Ghana, ensuring quick and culturally relevant assistance.
